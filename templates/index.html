  • Where it All Began

    New East. The site of my HIST128 recitation freshman year. Fall '21. It's a great building. Even better? It has amazing fire escapes. One on each side, and they're both painted. The easter side even has a landing.

    It became my goal to find every fire escape on campus and document their beauty. From there, the logical next step was chaos.
